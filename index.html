ABOUT US

Infernos Cricket Club (ICC), one of the finest cricket club based in Bangalore. ICC offers exhibition games, tournaments & training throughout the year.


Having a team of 35+ passionate players, we organize matches & tournaments on weekends and public holidays. ICC offers "Bangalore Premier Cup", a flagship tournament with an unique format which takes place once in a year; along with many tournaments scheduled bi-monthly.



ICC aspire in bringing the best out of each player. Young players learning and practicing with seniors & experienced coaches in the nets sessions & exhibition games, one couldn't ask for more. As a club, we aim at providing cricket training at state of the art facilities located in South Bangalore, for age group 17-25.

OUR ETHOS, VALUES & PHILOSOPHIES

Ethos/Mantra: We're Family (Mindset of Each Player). Whatever you lack, we got you & we will balance each other out. You need support, we will be your backbone. As long as you appreciate us and remain consistent, you don't ever have to doubt our loyalty. You got us, We got you!



Basis these values, good teams become great ones. Let's make Infernos a great team, together!

Our Philosophies:

1} Our Players: Our team is our players. We care for our player's health, wellbeinf & their personal development through the passion for Cricket. We believe that the game should be challeging and fun for every player.

2} Taking Responsibility: We take full responsibility for our own successes or failures. As a team, we recognize and celebrate our individual and collective successes & learn from failures.

3} Ownership: We believe that each player in our team should have the opportuity to share in the team's success. It is important that every team member takes onus of their responsibilities as if they are leading & own success.

4} Brightess of Future: We believe that our young talents have the right to belong to a team that will provide them with a exciting future and a supportive playing environment with learning edge, exposure and experience. They also have the right to see a clear pathway to achieving their cricketing goals.

5} Egalitarianism & Unity: In our team, we believe that each player should have equal privileges and rights. There will be no 'them' & 'us', but "we are one".

BANGALORE PREMIER CUP

Having represented in many tournaments around Bengaluru, we have realized that passion for cricket among people of all walks of life is enormous and growing each day especially amongst Corporate employees. Hence, we came up with an idea of “Three Cup Series - Bangalore Premier Cup” popularly known as BPC; which is a mixed corporate tournament. The first edition of BPC held in 2019 & it was a huge success where more than 24 teams participated and over 350 players got a chance to play in one of a kind tournament.



In the year 2020, we introduced shorter tournament formats of BPC such as Coexist and Blaze T-2O where more than 8 teams participated in each tournament. These tournament also received positive responses from various cricket clubs in Bangalore.
